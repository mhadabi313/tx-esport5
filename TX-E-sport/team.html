Our Team

Meet the core squad driving TX Esports in every tournament, practice block, and high-pressure qualifier. Each player brings a unique role, but all of them share the same standard: discipline, consistency, and complete trust in team play.

RAVEN

IGL / Captain

Master strategist with elite map control and confident shot-calling. Keeps the team organized in chaotic rounds and turns pressure moments into smart, winnable plays through timing and communication.

NYX

Entry Fragger

Aggressive opener creating space for the whole team with fast reads and fearless entries. Sets the pace of the round and gives teammates room to execute their utility and positioning without hesitation.

VOID

Support

Reliable utility specialist and round stabilizer who protects the team structure from early mistakes. Reads rotations, secures flanks, and keeps the tactical plan alive in difficult mid-round situations.

KRONOS

Sniper

Clutch performer with precise long-range aim and calm decision-making under pressure. Controls critical sightlines, punishes overextensions, and closes tight rounds with high-impact picks.

BLAZE

Duelist

Explosive mechanical player with fast peeks and high confidence in opening fights. Converts micro-advantages into full-site control and keeps pressure high through nonstop aggression.

ECHO

Flex

Adapts instantly between supportive and aggressive roles based on round flow. Bridges team communication and fills tactical gaps when unexpected situations break the default plan.

VEX

Controller

Utility timing expert who denies enemy information and isolates fights for teammates. Shapes map tempo with smart setups that force opponents into predictable rotations and mistakes.

ONYX

Anchor

Defensive specialist with excellent hold discipline and late-round patience. Absorbs pressure on weak-side positions, buys time for rotations, and secures critical clutch resets.

PHANTOM

Lurker

Silent map manipulator with elite timing on flank routes and backline pressure. Breaks enemy setups from behind and creates win conditions from low-information moments.

TITAN

Rifler

Consistent high-damage rifler who anchors mid-round duels with strong crosshair discipline. Delivers dependable impact in both executes and retakes across every map pool.
